Challenge One - Create an Equity Research Report

Equity research is at the heart of good decision making when it comes to selecting investible companies. And the best research on Wall Street employs channel checks – and that’s why we call this portal Channelchek. A channel check is when the research analyst gathers information from all available sources including probing into the company’s distribution channels; understanding the company from the perspective of their customers. So start with Channelchek and use every other resource available in our extremely resourceful world. Pick any company on Channelchek and start digging!

The winning report will follow the established Wall Street format including the following components: Business Description, Headline & Front-Page Bullet Points (executive summary), Investment Appraisal, Industry Overview & Competitive Position, Financial Analysis, Management & Corporate Governance Overview, Stock Valuation, and Risk Assessment.

The winner will be determined by a team of senior equity research analysts at Noble Capital Markets, based on scoring of each section of the report. Judges are FINRA (Financial Industry Regulatory Authority) certified Series 86, 87, and 7 analysts with industry accreditations including “Best on the Street” as determined by the Wall Street Journal Survey, Starmine ranked, and/or Institutional Investor (II) ranked. The analysts have an average of 27 years-experience in the equity markets.

Official rules and guidelines together with the list of prizes will be available in late November, 2018. Students are encouraged to register NOW and start formulating strategies. Registration closes December 21, 2018 with research report submissions due February 28, 2019; the winner will be announced March 27, 2019.
